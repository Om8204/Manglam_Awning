About Us

Our Company

Welcome to Manglam Awning , where innovation meets craftsmanship in the world of shade solutions. With a passion for creating functional and aesthetically pleasing spaces, we specialize in designing, manufacturing, and installing a diverse range of awnings and tensile structures that enhance the beauty and comfort of your environment.

Our Journey

Founded in [2017], Manglam Awning began with a vision to transform outdoor spaces into comfortable, stylish retreats. Our journey started with a small team of dedicated professionals who shared a common goal: to provide high-quality, customizable shade solutions that cater to the unique needs of our clients. Over the years, we have grown into a reputable name in the industry, known for our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction.
